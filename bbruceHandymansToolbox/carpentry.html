Lessons in woodworking

Let's talk safety

As you can see in the table to the right, many of the most common wood species you'll find yourself working with can cause some uncomfortable and potentially serious problems. There are some species out there such as Poison Walnut that actually have corrosive sap and Oleander can cause acute cardiac affects!

The moral of the story is this. Make sure that when working with wood, know it's species and it's affects on the human body. Always, always, always, wear proper protective gear. This includes gloves, dust mask, eye protection, and sometimes even long sleeves and an apron. Protect yourself, there's only one of you.
