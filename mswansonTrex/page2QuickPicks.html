Party Night!

Pick 3-4 Cheeses

  • Soft, spreadable cheese(Boursin, Montchevre, Allouette)
  • Harder Cheese(Parmiggiano Reggiano, Manchego, Deer Creek 7 Year Aged Cheddar)
  • Semi Soft(Marieke Gouda, Roth Havarti, Fontina)
  • Soft Ripened(Triple Cream Brie, Harbison)

Pick 3-4 Meats

  • Prosciutto
  • Finocchiona
  • Sopressata
  • Capicolla
  • Mortadella
  • Speck
  • Italian Dry Salami
  • Genoa Salami
