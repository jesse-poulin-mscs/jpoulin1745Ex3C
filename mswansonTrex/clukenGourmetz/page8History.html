History of Specialty Cheese and Charcuterie

History of Specialty Cheese:

History of Specialty Cheese: It is difficult to say when exactly cheese was first being produced. However, it is safe to say that the earliest cheeses were the hardest cheeses and were produced as a source of food to prevent spoilage. Cheeses were formed into wheels for ease of transportation so that cheese makers and customers could roll them on the ground.

History of Charcuterie

Similar to specialty cheese, Charcuterie did not start off being a luxury item. Charcuterie originated long before refrigeration technology was around. The method of curing meats was developed due to having a lack of access to something like refrigeration. When the meats were rubbed with salt, that salt would extract all of moisture contents which would in turn add a lot of flavor to the meat and it makes great snacks.
